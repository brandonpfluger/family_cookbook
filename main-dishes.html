Chili

Ingredients

1 bag of dried red kidney beans
2 tbs oil
2-3 cloves garlic, minced
½ onion, chopped
½ cup chopped bell pepper
1 lb. ground beef
1 small can tomato sauce
3 1 lb. cans stewed tomatoes
chili powder to taste
1 tsp salt
1 tsp oregano
1 tsp paprika

Directions

Soak the beans about 1-2 hours in pan. Wash beans. Bring to quick boil, rinse off water, and put in fresh, clean water (about 2 inches over beans). Salt the water and cook about 1½ hours. In oil brown meat, onion, garlic, and bell pepper. Add remaining ingredients and simmer about 30 minutes. Add the drained beans and continue to cook about 2 hours or until beans are tender.

Country Pot Roast

Ingredients

2 garlic cloves
1 5 lb. beef chuck cross rib pot roast (boneless or bottom rump round roast)
¼cup salad oil
1 cup tomato juice (V-8)
2 medium carrots
2 medium onions, cut up
1 cup thinly sliced celery
1 tbs salt
1 tsp oregano leaves
¼ tsp pepper

Directions

Crush the garlic and rub it onto the roast. On wax paper, coat the meat with flour. In a larg pot over medium heat, in hot oil, cook the pot roast until it is evenly browned on all sides. Add tomato juice and remaining ingredients. Heat to boiling, then reduce heat to low. Cover the pot and simmer for 4 hours or until meat is fork tender, turning it occasionally. Transfer meat to a platter. Fill the blender ¾ full with the liquid and vegetables from the pot. Cover and blend on high until all is blended. Do the same with the remainder of the juices in the pot. Return the blended mixture to the pot. Heat until boiling. Pour the gravy over the roast and serve.

Crumb Steak

Ingredients

1 pack of cubed steak or however many you want
2 eggs
salt and pepper
Italian bread crumbs

Directions

Scramble the eggs with a dash of salt and pepper in a dish big enough to dip the meat in. In another dish or plate, put some Italian bread crumbs on. Dip the meat in the egg mixture and then cover with the bread crumbs. Fry the meat in a pan with about ¼ high of oil until browned on both sides. Drain on paper towels.

Ham Hock and Beans

Ingredients

1 package beans
1 onion, finely chopped
worcestershire sauce
liquid smoke
ham hocks
2-3 carrots, chopped

Directions

Rinse beans and put into the pot. Fill pot with clean water, about 2-3 inches from top. Bring to boil. Rinse beans and refill with water. Add chopped onion, dash of worcestershire sauce, and liquid smoke. Put in ham hocks. Bring to boil, then turn to medium heat. Pepper to taste. When beans soften, put in carrots. When hocks soften, remove and let cool. Put meat and bone back with beans. Cook covered for 2-2½ hours.

Marinara Sauce

Ingredients

2 tbs olive oil or vegetable oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 16 oz. can of tomatoes
1 tbs sugar
1¼ tsp basil

Directions

Heat oil and cook garlic. Stir in the tomatoes, liquid, and the remaining ingredients. Simmer around 20 minutes.

Meatloaf

Ingredients

1 lb. - 1½ lbs. ground beef
1 carrot, grated
1 onion, grated
1 potato grated and soaked in water
1 tsp mustard
dash worcestershire sauce
salt and pepper to taste
1 tbs Italian bread crumbs
1 egg
1 8 oz. can of tomato sauce
3 squirts ketchup

Directions

In a bowl add meat, grated carrot, grated drained potato, onion, mustard, salt, pepper, worcestershire sauce, bread crumbs, and egg. Mix well. Form to loaf pan. Make 3 finger holes in the top of the meat. Cook at 350° fahrenheit for the first 10 minutes, then cook at 325° fahrenheit for the next 50 minutes. After about an hour, pour off fat from meat. Pour 8 oz. can of tomato sauce mixed with about 3 squirts of ketchup and a dash of water (about 1 tablespoon) on top of meat. Cook at 325° fahrenheit for another 30 minutes.

S.O.S.

Ingredients

1 big jar of armour chipped beef, shredded
1 heaping tbs margarine
2 tbs flour
pepper to taste
milk

Directions

Remember that this is a salty meat - DO NOT add salt. Put margarine in pan and melt on low heat. Add flour to melted margarine and mix well. Sprinkle with a little pepper. Let cook for about 5 minutes. Add milk a little at a time, stirring constantly. Try not to get lumps; if you do, work them out. If mixture is too thick, add a little more milk. Once gravy is desired consistency, add jar of chipped beef. Once heated through, it is ready to be served over rice or whatever else you like.

Shrimp Etouffee

Ingredients

2 lbs. fresh shrimp, peeled and de-veined
¼ cup oleo OR 3 tbs oil
1 cup onion, finely chopped
½ cup celery, finely chopped
½ cup finely chopped bell pepper
4 cloves garlic, pressed
1 tbs cornstarch
1½ cups water
salt
black pepper
cayenne pepper to taste

Directions

Split the shrimp and season generously with salt, black pepper, and cayenne. Set aside. Melt the oleo (margarine) or oil and add the onions, celery, bell pepper, and garlic. Cook slowly in an uncovered, heavy pot until the onions are wilted. Add the seasoned shrimp and let simmer, stirring occasionally, for about 20 minutes. Dissolve the cornstarch in water and add to the mixture. Cook another 15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Serve over cooked rice.

Stromboli Wraps

Ingredients

4 mission zesty garlic herb tortilla wraps
⅓ cup cup butter or margarine, softened
6 tbs shredded parmesan
4 oz. provolone, thinly sliced
4 oz. salami, thinly sliced
4 oz. capicolla or ham, thinly sliced
½ cup roasted bell pepper, rinsed and sliced
4 oz. mozzarella cheese, shredded
marinara sauce

Directions

Lightly spread butter on one side of wrap. Sprinkle with 1 tablespoon parmesan. Arrange ¼ each: provolone, salami, capicolla or ham, and peppers over center of wrap, leaving a border all around. Sprinkle with ¼ of mozzarella. Fold wrap. Place seam side down on pan. Brush with butter and sprinkle with remainder of parmesan. Bake at 400° fahrenheit for 12-15 minutes or until golden brown.

Stuffed Canneloni

Ingredients

1 28-32 oz. jar spaghetti sauce
½ cup water
½ lb. lean ground beef (80% lean)
¼ cup chopped onion
1-1½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
⅓ cup dry bread crumbs
1 egg, slightly beaten
¼ tsp Italian seasoning
1 3.75 oz. package Golden Grain/Mission Stuff-A-Roni

Directions

Heat oven to 375° fahrenheit. Combine spaghetti sauce and water. Pour half of sauce mixture into bottom of 11 x 7-inch baking dish. Set aside. Brown ground beef and onion; drain. Combine beef mixture, ½ cup cheese, reserving 1 cup cheese and next 3 ingredients. Mix well. Fill uncooked Stuff-A-Roni using a rounded knife or teaspoon. Arrange in a single layer in baking dish and top evenly with remaining sauce and filling. Cover tightly with foil and bake for 1 hour. Uncover; top with remaining cheese. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.

Taco Pie

Ingredients

1 package Pilsbury crescent roll
1 lb. ground beef
1 Old El Paso taco seasoning mix
2 small cans tomato sauce
grated mild cheddar
diced tomatoes
sliced black olives
shredded lettuce
whatever else you may want to top with

Directions

Spread crescent rolls on pizza pan to make pizza crust. Cook as directed after you poke the dough with fork. While dough is cooking, brown hamburger meat. Add tomato sauce and seasoning mix. After dough is cooked, add meat sauce and cheese over top. Put back in oven only to melt cheese. Remove and add rest of ingredients on top.

Yorkshire Pudding

Ingredients

1 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup milk
2 eggs
½ tsp salt
¼ cup drippings from meat

Directions

Mix all ingredients except drippings until smooth. Heat oven to 425° fahrenheit. Place drippings in heated square pan. Pour batter into pan. Bake until puffed and golden brown (about 25 minutes).
