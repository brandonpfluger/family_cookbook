Griddle Salsa Roja

Ingredients

10-12 Roma tomatoes
1 jalapeño
1 large yellow onion (sliced into rings so you char them easier)
5 garlic cloves
1 chipotle pepper in adobo sauce, plus one tablespoon of the sauce
1 tbs vegetable oil
1 bunch cilantro
1 tbs lime juice
salt to taste (about 2 tablespoons)

Directions

Heat a large skillet on the stove (cast iron if you have one). In the hot, dry pan, char tomatoes, jalapeño, onion, and garlic. You want the vegetables to have the black char marks; that gives the salsa flavor. After you have charred the vegetables, use a food processor to chop all the vegetables and the rest of the ingredients. Process in 2 or 3 batches. Add the salt at the end. You will want to add some water to make it thinner.

Potato Salad

Ingredients

1 cup mayo
½ cup sour cream
celery salt
½ cup chopped celery
black olives, sliced
1 tsp sweet relish
mustard to taste
salt and pepper to taste
hardboiled egg, sliced
8-10 new potatoes (white skin)

Directions

Boil potatoes with skin on until fork tender. Drain, cool, and peel of skin. Mix mayo, sour cream, and mustard together. Chunk potatoes in bowl and mix in mayo mixture; stir. Sprinkle with celery salt, salt, and pepper; mix well. Add celery, olives, and relish. Once mixed well and flavored to taste, add sliced eggs to decorate top.

Split Pea Soup

Ingredients

1 bag split peas
3 tbs oil
1 onion, finely chopped
salt and pepper to taste
diced carrots (as many as you'd like)
diced ham (as much as you prefer)

Directions

Rinse peas thoroughly and put them in pan and fill with water. Add the oil and finely chopped onion. Salt and pepper to taste. When peas are tender, dice ham and carrots and cook until done (3-4 hours).
